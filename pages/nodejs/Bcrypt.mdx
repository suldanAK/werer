1. ### bcrypt  

bcrypt waa library ama maktabad loo isticmaalo inay si ammaan ah u sameyso hashing-ka iyo encryption-ka furahaaga sirta ah (passwords). Tani waxay ka hortagtaa in furahaaga
 sirta ah lagu kaydiyo qaab aan ammaan ahayn, taasoo ka ilaalinaysa haddii xogtaada la jabsado.
2. ### bcrypt.hash:

bcrypt.hash waa method ka mid ah bcrypt oo loo isticmaalo inuu sameeyo hashing-ka furaha sirta ah. Hashing wuxuu u badalayaa furaha sirta ah mid aan la arki karin oo aan dib
 loogu rogi karin (one-way encryption). Taasi macnaheedu waa marka furaha sirta ah la hash gareeyo, waxaad isticmaashaa shaashadda cusub ee loo yaqaan 'hashed password'.
3. ### password:

password waa furaha sirta ah ee isticmaalehu geliyo, taas oo aad rabto inaad u hash gareyso. Waa variable ka kooban string-ka furaha sirta ah ee loo beddelayo hash.
4.  ### saltRounds:

saltRounds wuxuu tilmaamayaa tirada wareegyada (rounds) ama heerka 'salting'. Salt waa wax laga daro furaha sirta ah si loo abuuro hash gaar ah oo aad u adag, xitaa haddii 
furayaasha sirta ah ay isku mid yihiin. saltRounds wuxuu sare u qaadaa heerka amniga, laakiin wuxuu kordhiyaa xawaaraha hawlgalka (waqtiga loo baahan yahay si loo abuuro hash-ka).
5. ### await:

await waxaa loo isticmaalaa si loo sugo natiijada function-kaan bcrypt.hash ka hor intaadan u gudbin tallaabooyinka xiga. Tan waxaa loo adeegsadaa si looga shaqeeyo hab 
asynchronous (oo aan qasab ahayn inay sugto howsha hash-gareynta), taasoo hagaajinaysa waxqabadka.


### Sida uu u shaqeeyo:
Markaad wacdo bcrypt.hash, waxa uu qaataa furaha sirta ah iyo tirada wareegyada 'salt' si uu u sameeyo hash.
Bcrypt wuxuu soo saaraa furaha sirta ah ee la hash gareeyay (hashed password).
Hash-kaan waxaa la keydin karaa oo loo isticmaali karaa marka isticmaaluhu isku dayo inuu galo, isagoo barbardhigaya hash-ka keydsan iyo kan cusub ee uu soo geliyo.
### Faa'iido:

Habkaani wuxuu ka hortagayaa in furaha sirta ah si sahlan loo jabsado ama loo garto, xitaa haddii xogtaadu gasho gacmo khaldan.


# waa maxay bcrypt.compare


`bcrypt.compare` waa habka loo isticmaalo si loo xaqiijiyo haddii erayga sirta ah ee user-ku soo geliyo uu la mid yahay hash-kii markii hore loo keydiyey. Marka user-ku eray
 sir ah galiyo, laguma keydiyo qaabka asalka ah (plaintext), ee waxaa loo beddelaa hash, ka dibna hashkaas ayaa la keydiyaa.

Marka erayga sirta ah dib loo xaqiijiyo (authentication), habka` bcrypt.compare` ayaa la isticmaalaa si loo hubiyo haddii hashka keydsan uu la mid yahay erayga sirta ah ee 
user-ku soo geliyo markaas.


## Sida `bcrypt.compare `u shaqeeyo:
Erayga sirta ah ee user-ku geliyo: Marka user-ka laga doonayo inuu dib u galo, wuxuu gelinayaa eray sir ah oo caadi ah (plaintext password).

Hashka la keydiyey: Tani waa hash erayga sirta ah ee markii hore lagu sameeyey farsamada` bcrypt.hash `lana kaydiyey database-ka.

Barbardhiga: `bcrypt.compare` waxay qaadataa labada eray:

Erayga user-ku soo geliyo (plaintext).
Hashka la kaydiyey (waxay noqon kartaa hash hore loo sameeyey oo database-ka ku keydsan).
Habka shaqada: Bcrypt waxay isticmaashaa "salting" iyo hab kale oo farsamo si ay u tijaabiso haddii hashka laga soo saari karo erayga sirta ah ee user-ku geliyey. Waxay isticmaashaa habka isla markaas "hashing" 
si loo arko haddii natiijadu u dhiganto hashkii la keydiyay.
